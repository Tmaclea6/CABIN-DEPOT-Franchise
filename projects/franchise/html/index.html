Become a Franchisee

As Canada's largest off-grid living solutions provider, The Cabin Depot™ is looking for like-minded business owners and entrepreneurs who are interested in owning their own business, offering a dynamic range of off-grid products & exclusive brands, and providing exceptional service to their customers.

PREREQUISITES:

Owning and operating any business can be both challenging and rewarding, and we are looking specifically for potential franchise store owners who must:
■ Be passionate about customer service
■ Be motivated to succeed, and be directly involved with store operations
■ Possess a strong business acumen, with a general understanding of off-grid systems and equipment
■ Have a minimum of 1500SF of showroom space available
■ Required initial investment: $200k to $400k depending on demographics of the area being serviced
While not a specific requirement, preference is given to those who currently operate an installation-based business related to Propane, HVAC, or Solar PV.

The Opportunity

As the first franchise system of its kind, The Cabin Depot now offers franchise opportunities across Canada. We are excited to bring our tried & tested system to your local market with a unique blend of quality products, interactive displays, and support under one roof.

Growing Market

Canadians are increasingly wanting to escape their busy urban lives & spend more time at their off-grid retreats. Our extensive experience, supported by actual data, shows the growing movement and interest in the off-grid lifestyle.

Exclusive Brands

We have spent more than a decade building strong partnerships with the world's leading names in off-grid products. This has resulted in numerous exclusive agreements with both manufacturers and suppliers. These arrangements have led to the development of our own branded products exclusive to The Cabin Depot.
